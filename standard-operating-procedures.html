Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

STAC Marine Offshore maintains robust and comprehensive Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) governing all operational and maintenance activities. These procedures form an integral part of the STAC Management System (SMS) and are designed to ensure safe, consistent, and efficient execution of work across all operations.

All personnel, including employees, contractors, and third-party service providers, are required to strictly comply with the Management System when carrying out any activity for or on behalf of STAC Marine Offshore. Adherence to approved SOPs is mandatory and critical to maintaining operational integrity, safety, and regulatory compliance.

The STAC Management System is subject to periodic review and continuous improvement to ensure ongoing alignment with industry best practices, technological advancements, applicable local regulations, classification society requirements, and lessons learned from operational experience.

Through the consistent application and continuous enhancement of our SOPs, STAC Marine Offshore reinforces its commitment to operational excellence, safety, and compliance across all activities.
