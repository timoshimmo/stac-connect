25 June 2025

Technical Workshop - Aligning Strategy and Driving Excellence

- Corporate

Important updates to our corporate travel policy have been implemented. All employees should review the new guidelines before booking their next business trip. These changes reflect our commitment to employee wellbeing, cost efficiency, and environmental responsibility.

Major Policy Changes

The updated policy introduces several significant changes to how we approach business travel globally. These modifications have been carefully considered based on employee feedback, industry best practices, and our corporate sustainability goals.

Updated Guidelines

  • Business class now approved for flights over 6 hours (previously 8 hours)
  • Increased daily meal allowance by 15% across all regions
  • New carbon offset program for all company-funded travel
  • Flexible work arrangements available during extended business trips
  • Enhanced travel insurance coverage including pandemic-related disruptions
  • Rail travel encouraged for routes under 4 hours as a sustainable alternative

Sustainability Initiatives

As part of our commitment to reducing our environmental footprint, we're introducing new guidelines that encourage sustainable travel choices. When rail travel is a viable option for journeys under 4 hours, it should be selected as the preferred mode of transportation. The company will also offset 100% of carbon emissions from all business flights through certified environmental programs.

Duty of Care

Employee safety and wellbeing remain our top priority. The enhanced travel insurance now covers a wider range of scenarios including pandemic-related travel disruptions, political unrest, and natural disasters. All travelers will receive pre-trip safety briefings for high-risk destinations.

Expense Reimbursement

The reimbursement process has been simplified with higher daily allowances that eliminate the need for individual meal receipts in most cases. Employees can now claim the standard daily rate without submitting itemized meal receipts, reducing administrative burden.

These policy updates will take effect on October 1, 2022. Please review the complete policy document available on the company portal and contact HR with any questions or concerns.
