Emergency Preparedness Procedure

At STAC Marine Offshore, a robust and comprehensive Emergency Response Plan (ERP) is in place to ensure effective preparedness for all credible emergency scenarios that could impact our operations, personnel, or assets.

The ERP clearly identifies potential emergencies relevant to our operations and defines the resources, systems, and controls required to respond effectively. Roles and responsibilities are formally assigned to personnel based on the specific duties they are required to perform during emergencies, ensuring clarity, coordination, and timely response.

To maintain a high level of readiness, emergency drills and exercises are conducted regularly to test our response capability, validate procedures, and reinforce competence across the organization. Lessons learned from drills and real events are reviewed and incorporated into the ERP as part of our continuous improvement process.

Through structured planning, defined accountability, and regular testing, STAC Marine Offshore remains committed to protecting people, the environment, and assets, and to responding decisively and effectively to any emergency situation.
