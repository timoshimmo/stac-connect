OUR BEHAVIOUR - VALUES

To truly live by our core values, Stac Marine expects all employees to exhibit the following behaviors, which reflect our commitment to sustaining a high-performing, ethical, and collaborative workplace:

Sustainability

  • Actively reduce waste and energy consumption in both office and offshore environments.
  • Proactively identify and address sustainability challenges.
  • Use resources efficiently, avoiding waste.
  • Strictly adhere to environmental laws and regulations.
  • Continually seek to enhance knowledge on sustainable practices.

Teamwork

  • Communicate openly and honestly across teams.
  • Collaborate and offer assistance to colleagues to achieve team goals.
  • Trust and support each team member's contributions.
  • Resolve conflicts respectfully, focusing on solutions.
  • Recognize and celebrate team achievements.

Accountability

  • Take full responsibility for all tasks, decisions, and outcomes.
  • Admit mistakes promptly and take corrective action.
  • Execute duties reliably and deliver on commitments.
  • Hold others accountable in a constructive manner.
  • Maintain high ethical standards in all business activities.

Competence

  • Engage in continuous learning and skill development.
  • Share knowledge and expertise to foster collective competence.
  • Seek and utilize feedback to improve performance.
  • Deliver high-quality work with attention to detail and safety.
  • Apply expertise to solve problems and drive innovation.

OUR BEHAVIOUR - ETHICS

The following ethical guidelines represent the foundation of how we conduct ourselves at Stac Marine Offshore:

Honesty and Integrity

Every employee is expected to act with honesty and integrity in all business dealings. This means being truthful in our communications, transparent in our actions, and maintaining trust in our relationships with colleagues, clients, and partners.

Fairness and Equity

We are committed to treating all individuals fairly and with respect, ensuring that decisions are free from bias or favoritism. Employees are expected to uphold fairness in all interactions, from project assignments to promotions and performance evaluations.

Compliance with Laws and Regulations

Stac Marine Offshore operates within the framework of all applicable laws and regulations. Employees are required to understand and comply with the legal requirements relevant to their roles, particularly those governing offshore operations, environmental standards, safety protocols, and labor laws.

Confidentiality and Data Protection

Employees must safeguard all confidential information related to the company, its clients, and its partners. This includes protecting proprietary information, intellectual property, client data, and any internal communications. Breaching confidentiality undermines trust and can have legal consequences.

Conflicts of Interest

Employees are expected to avoid any activities, relationships, or situations that could create a conflict between personal interests and the interests of Stac Marine. If a potential conflict arises, it must be disclosed immediately to management to ensure transparency and resolution.

LEADERSHIP PRINCIPLES

At Stac Marine, everyone is a leader. Leadership at Stac Marine is about empowering others while upholding the values of the organization. Our leaders are expected to:

  • Lead by Example: Demonstrate commitment to the company's values, especially in terms of accountability, transparency, and competence.
  • Inclusive Decision-Making: Engage teams in discussions, encouraging participation and collective input while ensuring decisions align with the company's objectives.
  • Provide Guidance and Support: Mentor team members, fostering professional development and ensuring the success of their teams.
  • Ensure Consistency: Apply company policies and procedures consistently and fairly, ensuring all employees are treated equitably.
  • Foster a Feedback-Driven Culture: Encourage a culture of open feedback where leaders and employees alike engage in constructive dialogues for continuous improvement.

DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION COMMITMENT

Stac Marine is committed to fostering a workplace that respects and values diverse perspectives, backgrounds, and experiences. We believe that diversity and inclusion are essential to innovation and operational excellence. To uphold this commitment:

  • Every employee deserves equal opportunities regardless of race, gender, age, religion, or background.
  • There are no biases in decision-making; we ensure that merit and contribution drive opportunities.
  • Cultural sensitivity will be upheld always to ensure that all employees feel respected and valued.
  • We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy toward discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind. We take immediate action when such issues arise, ensuring a safe and respectful work environment for all employees.
  • We are committed to ensuring that career advancement opportunities are accessible to all employees, regardless of background. Promotions and advancements are based solely on merit, performance, and contributions.
