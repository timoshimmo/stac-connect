12 December 2024

Travel booking routines

- Nigeria

Please take note of the following routines when booking your travels with ATPI. These updated procedures ensure smooth travel arrangements for all employees across our Norway, France, and Netherlands offices.

Booking Process

All travel bookings must be made through our designated travel partner ATPI. The booking system is accessible 24/7 through the company portal. Please ensure you have all necessary approvals before initiating your booking.

Key Requirements

  • Book flights at least 14 days in advance for domestic travel and 21 days for international trips
  • Select economy class for flights under 6 hours duration
  • Use preferred hotel partners when available at your destination
  • Submit expense reports within 30 days of travel completion
  • Retain all receipts and documentation for reimbursement

Approval Workflow

The approval process has been streamlined to ensure faster processing times. Your direct manager must approve all travel requests, with additional CFO approval required for international trips exceeding €2,000. Emergency travel requests should be flagged accordingly in the system.

Support and Assistance

If you encounter any issues or have questions about the booking process, please contact the ATPI support team at travel@company.com or call the dedicated helpline available in your region. The team is available Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM local time.

Thank you for your cooperation in following these procedures. These guidelines help us manage travel costs effectively while ensuring your safety and comfort during business trips.

20 September 2024

Travel booking – new procedure

- Nigeria, Global

Good News! You can now book your own travels. This is applicable for employees in Norway, France, and the Netherlands. We're excited to announce this new self-service capability that gives you more control over your business travel arrangements.

What's New?

Previously, all travel bookings had to be processed through the central travel desk. Starting today, you can directly book your flights, hotels, and rental cars through our new online booking platform. This change is designed to give you more flexibility and speed up the booking process.

Getting Started

  • Access the new booking platform through the company intranet
  • Log in using your standard company credentials
  • Complete the quick 15-minute online training module (mandatory)
  • Review the updated travel policy guidelines
  • Start booking your next business trip!

Benefits of Self-Booking

The new system offers several advantages including instant booking confirmation, real-time price comparisons, access to preferred vendor rates, automatic expense integration, and mobile app access for booking on the go. You'll also receive immediate notifications about any changes to your travel arrangements.

Policy Compliance

While you now have the freedom to book your own travel, all company travel policies still apply. The system includes built-in guardrails to ensure compliance with budget limits, preferred vendors, and advance booking requirements. Any exceptions will require manager approval before confirmation.

Training and Support

Live training sessions are scheduled for the next two weeks. Register through the HR portal to join a session convenient for your time zone. Additionally, comprehensive video tutorials and a detailed user guide are available on the travel portal.

We're confident this new system will enhance your travel booking experience. Please don't hesitate to reach out to the support team if you need any assistance during the transition.

15 September 2024

Corporate travel policy updates

- Global

Important updates to our corporate travel policy have been implemented. All employees should review the new guidelines before booking their next business trip. These changes reflect our commitment to employee wellbeing, cost efficiency, and environmental responsibility.

Major Policy Changes

The updated policy introduces several significant changes to how we approach business travel globally. These modifications have been carefully considered based on employee feedback, industry best practices, and our corporate sustainability goals.

Updated Guidelines

  • Business class now approved for flights over 6 hours (previously 8 hours)
  • Increased daily meal allowance by 15% across all regions
  • New carbon offset program for all company-funded travel
  • Flexible work arrangements available during extended business trips
  • Enhanced travel insurance coverage including pandemic-related disruptions
  • Rail travel encouraged for routes under 4 hours as a sustainable alternative

Sustainability Initiatives

As part of our commitment to reducing our environmental footprint, we're introducing new guidelines that encourage sustainable travel choices. When rail travel is a viable option for journeys under 4 hours, it should be selected as the preferred mode of transportation. The company will also offset 100% of carbon emissions from all business flights through certified environmental programs.

Duty of Care

Employee safety and wellbeing remain our top priority. The enhanced travel insurance now covers a wider range of scenarios including pandemic-related travel disruptions, political unrest, and natural disasters. All travelers will receive pre-trip safety briefings for high-risk destinations.

Expense Reimbursement

The reimbursement process has been simplified with higher daily allowances that eliminate the need for individual meal receipts in most cases. Employees can now claim the standard daily rate without submitting itemized meal receipts, reducing administrative burden.

These policy updates will take effect on October 1, 2022. Please review the complete policy document available on the company portal and contact HR with any questions or concerns.
